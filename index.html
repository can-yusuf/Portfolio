About Me

I am a foreign policy expert specializing in Middle Eastern affairs, currently serving as Program Coordinator for the Middle East Program at The Wilson Center. My work centers on driving impactful dialogue, leading research initiatives, and bridging stakeholders across sectors.

I hold an M.A. in Democracy and Governance from Georgetown University and a B.A. in Political Science from California State University, Sacramento.
