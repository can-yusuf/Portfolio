About Me

I am a dedicated professional in political science and Middle Eastern affairs, currently serving as the Program Coordinator for the Middle East Program at The Wilson Center. My work focuses on fostering impactful discussions, leading research initiatives, and connecting stakeholders across various sectors.

Previously, I completed my M.A. in Democracy and Governance at Georgetown University and my B.A. in Political Science at California State University, Sacramento.
